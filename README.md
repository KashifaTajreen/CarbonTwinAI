# 🌍 Carbon Twin AI
### Generative Digital Twin for Personal Sustainability Planning

Carbon Twin AI is an AI-powered sustainability platform that creates a **digital twin of an individual's lifestyle**, predicts their future carbon footprint, and simulates alternative greener futures.

Unlike traditional carbon calculators that simply display emissions, Carbon Twin AI allows users to experiment with different lifestyle scenarios and understand how small changes can significantly impact their environmental future.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Design a solution that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through simple actions and personalized insights.

Most existing solutions:
- Provide generic tips
- Require manual calculations
- Lack personalization
- Do not predict future environmental impact

Carbon Twin AI addresses these limitations by combining **Machine Learning**, **Generative AI**, and **Scenario Simulation**.

---

## 💡 Solution Overview

Carbon Twin AI creates a virtual representation (Digital Twin) of a user's lifestyle using data such as:

- Transportation habits
- Food consumption
- Electricity usage
- Shopping patterns
- Consumption behavior

The platform then:

1. Estimates current carbon footprint
2. Predicts future emissions using Machine Learning
3. Simulates multiple greener lifestyle scenarios
4. Generates personalized sustainability roadmaps using Generative AI

## 🔥 Key Features

### 🌍 Digital Carbon Twin
Creates an AI representation of an individual's environmental behavior.

### 📈 Carbon Footprint Prediction
Uses a Random Forest model to estimate future carbon emissions based on lifestyle patterns.

### 🔮 Future Scenario Simulation
Simulates multiple possibilities, including:

- Increased public transportation usage
- Reduced meat consumption
- Green lifestyle transition

### 🧠 Gemini Sustainability Coach
Generates:
- Personalized recommendations
- Environmental impact analysis
- Sustainability roadmaps
- Action plans

### 📊 Sustainability Score
Provides an easy-to-understand sustainability metric for users.
